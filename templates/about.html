About Us

This Event Feedback System is developed as a digital initiative by our students to promote transparency, encourage student participation, and continuously enhance the quality of campus activities. By gathering valuable insights from attendees, we aim to create more meaningful and impactful experiences for the UEC community.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of student voices to shape campus life. Our platform provides a transparent and efficient way for students to share their experiences, helping organizers understand what works and what needs improvement. Together, we're building a more responsive campus community where every event becomes an opportunity for growth and connection.
